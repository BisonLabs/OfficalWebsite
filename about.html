Zero-Knowledge on BTC

Zero-Knowledge Proofs & ZK-STARKs

Zero-Knowledge Proofs & ZK-STARKs are like secret handshakes in the world of cryptography. They let you confirm something's true without giving away any other secrets. ZK-STARKs are a special kind of these handshakes, able to handle more information.

Bison's Use of ZK-STARKs

Think of Bison moving money around and using ZK-STARKs as a seal of approval. It's like a stamp on a letter that says, "This is legit," without telling you what's inside.
